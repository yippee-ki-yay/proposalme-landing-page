What is this?

. . . . . . . . . .

The main purpose of this platform is to provide a way for the freelancer to create beautiful and customizable proposals and start interacting with clients immediately. When you create and customize your proposal, you will get notified when the client has opened your proposal and you can start chatting with them immediately.
