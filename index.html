What is this?

. . . . . . . . . .

The main purpose of this platform is to provide a way for the freelancer to create beautiful and customizable proposals and start interacting with clients immediately. When you create your proposal and send it to your potential client, you will get notified when the client has opened your proposal and you can start chatting with them immediately.

Beautiful proposals

You can create custom greating messages for the client, choose different templates and upload your sketches and even videos.

Start right away

One of the key features of the platform is the on site text and video chat. You'll get a notification as soon as client opens your proposal and you can start chatting immidiately

Metrics and more

You can save different proposal templates and see which work the best, how many times have clients opened it and how they interacted with it.
